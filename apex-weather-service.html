🌪

Tornado Warning

Radar-confirmed or spotter-confirmed tornadoes. Immediate threat to life — alerts include polygon, affected counties, and storm ETA.

⚠ Warning
⛈

Severe Thunderstorm

Winds 58+ mph or hail 1"+ diameter. Includes storm cell movement, speed, and highest threat tags like CONSIDERABLE HAIL.

⚠ Warning
🌊

Flash Flood Warning

Life-threatening flooding in progress. Alerts detail affected waterways, expected accumulation, and hourly rainfall rates.

Tornado Watch

Conditions favorable for tornado development. Watch boxes posted with full county lists and expiration times.

Severe T-Storm Watch

Elevated risk of severe storms across a large area. Posted hours in advance so you can prepare before the storms arrive.

Server Rules
Keep It Safe
& Accurate
01
No false alarm panic. Do not spread unverified reports. Only link official NWS sources in alert channels.
02
Respect severity levels. Don't downplay or exaggerate active warnings. Lives depend on accurate information.
03
Keep alert channels clean. Chat belongs in #storm-chat. Alert channels are for bot posts and official updates only.
04
Be helpful to newcomers. Many members join during active events. Be patient, be accurate, be kind.
05
No location sharing pressure. Never ask members to share exact locations during emergencies.
